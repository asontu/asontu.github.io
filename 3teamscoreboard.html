Period

3-team Roller Derby Scoreboard

Set-up

There is no "controls" screen, the operator views the scoreboard as-is, mirrored on a projector. Press [shift]+[?] to show/hide this help-screen.

Set the desired period-time in the "period" box and the desired half-time length in the "Line-up" box. Fill in the names for the teams. Press [space] or click on the "Line-up" to start the first Line-up time. The scoreboard never goes from "Line-up" to "Jam is on" or vice versa automatically, you have to press [space].

During game

[space]Cycles between Line-up and Jam is on until end of period is reached. After the first period half-time starts ticking and a [space] will start the 30 second line-up for the 2nd period. After the 2nd period, [space] will show Final score.
[Esc]Cycles between Official Time-out and Team Time-out unless an Official Time-out is longer than a minute. You could press [space] to start a Line-up and then press [Esc] twice to start a new Team time-out.
[O]Only available during Official or Team Time-out. Goes to Official Review.
[1-0]Adds 1 through 10 seconds to the period clock. Or when [Ctrl] is helt down, subtracts 1 through 10 seconds from the period clock.
[E] [R]Respectively substracts/adds a point for team 1.
[D] [F]Respectively substracts/adds a point for team 2.
[C] [V]Respectively substracts/adds a point for team 3.
[P]Cycles between 1st period and 2nd period.
[?]Hides/shows this screen
