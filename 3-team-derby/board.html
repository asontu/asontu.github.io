3-team Roller Derby Scoreboard

This Roller Derby Scoreboard can display the score for 3 teams. It works completely on the client without any server. LocalStorage is used to save & restore information about the ongoing game, including continuing if the restored game was saved recent enough.

There is currently no "controls" screen, the operator views the scoreboard as-is, mirrored on a projector. Press ([shift] +) [?] to show/hide this help-screen.

Set-up

Set the desired period-time in the "Period 1" box and the desired halftime length in the "Halftime length" box. Fill in the names for the teams. Press [Space] to start the first Line-up time. The scoreboard never goes from "Line-up" to "Jam is on" or vice versa automatically, you have to press [Space].

During game

An overview of hotkey mapping is presented on the right. Move your mouse over the buttons to see how they relate.

[Space]"Normal" progression of the game, meaning it goes from set-up to the first Line-up and then cycles between Line-up and Jam is on until end of period is reached. After the first period half-time starts ticking and a [Space] will start the 30 second line-up for the 2nd period. After the 2nd period, [Space] will show Unofficial score and one more [Space] will go to Final score.
[Esc]"Deviation" from normal game progression, meaning it starts an Official Time-out and then cycles between Official Time-out and Team Time-out unless an Official Time-out is longer than a minute. You could press [Space] to start a Line-up and then press [Esc] twice to start a new Team time-out. During half-time, [Esc] will switch back to the 1st period to allow for one more jam, but be sure to add at least 1 second to the period clock for [Space] to start Line-up and not restart halftime. The same goes for the end of period 2.
[O]Goes to Official Review. Only available during Official or Team Time-out.
[P]Cycles between 1st period and 2nd period.
[Ctrl+Z] [Cmd+Z]Undo the last action from one of the above (Jam start, Time-out start, OR start, Period switch). You can undo up to 10 actions.
[Ctrl+Shift+Z] [Cmd+Shift+Z]Redo the last action that was undone. You can redo up to 10 actions so long as you don't start a new action with [Space], [Esc], [O] or [P].
[1]-[0]Adds 1 through 10 seconds to the period clock. Or when [Shift] is helt down, subtracts 1 through 10 seconds from the period clock.
[E] [R]Respectively substracts/adds a point for team 1.
[D] [F]Respectively substracts/adds a point for team 2.
[C] [V]Respectively substracts/adds a point for team 3.
[W] [T]Respectively substracts/adds 6 points for team 1.
[S] [G]Respectively substracts/adds 6 points for team 2.
[X] [B]Respectively substracts/adds 6 points for team 3.
[Ctrl+Esc]Completely reset game, only team-names and length of period/halftime are kept. This cannot be undone! It prompts for confirmation first, but this does halt the running clocks (known issue).
[?]Hides/shows this screen
