3-team Roller Derby Scoreboard

This Roller Derby Scoreboard can display the score for 3 teams. It works completely on the client without any server. LocalStorage is used to save & restore information about the ongoing game, including continuing if the restored game was saved recent enough.

There is currently no "controls" screen, the operator views the scoreboard as-is, mirrored on a projector. Press (or ) to show/hide this help-screen.

Set-up

Set the desired period-time in the "Period 1" box and the desired halftime length in the "Halftime length" box. Fill in the names for the teams. Press to start the first Line-up time. The scoreboard never goes from "Line-up" to "Jam is on" or vice versa automatically, you have to press .

During game

An overview of hotkey mapping is presented on the right. Move your mouse over the buttons to see how they relate.

"Normal" progression of the game, meaning it goes from set-up to the first Line-up and then cycles between Line-up and Jam is on until end of period is reached. After the first period Half-time starts ticking and now a will start the 30 second Line-up for the 2nd period. After the 2nd period, will show Unofficial score and one more will go to Final score.
"Deviation" from normal game progression, meaning it starts an Official Time-out and then cycles between Official Time-out and Team Time-out unless the Official Time-out is longer than a minute. You could press to start a Line-up and then press twice to start a new Team Time-out. During Half-time, will switch back to the 1st period to allow for one more jam, but be sure to add at least 1 second to the period clock for to start Line-up and not restart Half-time. The same goes for the end of period 2.
